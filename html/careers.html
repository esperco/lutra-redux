Available Positions

Full-Stack Developer

We're looking for a full-stack developer to help our users better manage their time. Esper is currently a small team, so you'll be involved in every aspect of product design, planning, and development.

We currently use OCaml, TypeScript, React, LESS, MySQL, Redis, and a lot of Amazon Web Services. We don't require prior experience with these technologies, only a willingness to learn. We're also open to experimenting with new tools.

Requirements

  • You have a basic understandin of how functional programming works and are comfortable with Javascript's various ... quirks.
  • You can write clean, testable code (and actual tests).
  • You have an eye for good design and pay attention to the little things.
  • You're willing to take ownership of a feature and make sure it ships.
  • You've thought a lot about time management and how to improve it.
  • You're okay with color purple.

Bonus Points

  • You've worked with React Native or developed mobile apps.
  • You've worked in a startup environment or have side projects of your own.
